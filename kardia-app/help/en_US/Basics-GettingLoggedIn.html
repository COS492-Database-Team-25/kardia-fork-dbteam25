Kardia Basics: Getting Logged In

Included are the basics of getting logged in to Kardia on your system



  1. Start Kardia from the icon on the desktop


    If you don't have a Kardia shortcut on your
    desktop,contact your IT System Administrator.

  2. Enter your user name and password in the Authentication box. Click OK.

    This will load the main Kardia screen.
